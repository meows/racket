Operations in Math

You may have heard the term "operations" or "operators" in math. If not, they look like +, -, ×, and ÷. An operator is just a function that gets special privilege of looking visually different. Even the equal sign = can be considered an operator.

In Racket, there are no operators

Hooray! In Racket, unless you are doing something unusual, we always put the function in front so we don't have to think about order of operations as much.
