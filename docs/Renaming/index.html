Rename anything

In Racket you're allowed to rename anything, even the way you define variables and functions:

#lang racket

(require (rename-in racket/base [define fn]))
(require (rename-in racket/base [define def]))

(fn (add2 x) (+ x 2))
(def age 10)
(add2 age) ;; 12

(fn ++ add1)
(fn -- sub1)

(-- 3) ;; 2
(-- 2) ;; 1
(++ 1) ;; 2
(++ 2) ;; 3
(~> 2 ++ ++)
(~> 2 -- ++ -- ++)
